How to Play Connect 4

Game Rules

Connect 4 is a two-player connection game in which the players take turns dropping colored discs into a seven-column, six-row vertically suspended grid.

Players

Two players: Red and Yellow

Taking Turns

Players alternate turns, dropping one disc per turn into any column that isn't full

Winning

The first player to connect four of their discs horizontally, vertically, or diagonally wins

Pro Tips

  • Control the center columns - they offer more winning opportunities
  • Watch for your opponent's potential winning moves
  • Create multiple threats at once to force a win
  • Don't forget about diagonal connections!

Controls

Click on a column to drop your disc

Tap on mobile devices

Reset button starts a new game
